Cosmetic Insights and Consumer Analysis Platform

This platform provides an interactive visualization system for analyzing cosmetic product data. It helps understand consumer behavior, product performance, brand rankings, and skin suitability. Using Tableau dashboards integrated into a web interface, users can explore cosmetic trends, compare products, and gain valuable insights to support business and research decisions.

About the Project

The Cosmetic Insights Analysis project focuses on transforming raw cosmetic product data into meaningful visual insights using Tableau dashboards integrated with a web interface.

The system analyzes important factors such as product rankings, brand performance, product pricing, and skin suitability. These insights help businesses understand market trends and customer preferences.

The project demonstrates the integration of Tableau dashboards with web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, providing an interactive and user-friendly environment.

This solution helps cosmetic companies, analysts, and researchers make data-driven decisions to improve product quality and marketing strategies.

Data Story and Analysis

Data storytelling combines visualization and narrative to explain patterns and trends clearly.

This dashboard explains how cosmetic products perform across different brands and categories.

  • Comparison of product rankings across brands
  • Analysis of skin suitability for cosmetic products
  • Relationship between price and performance
  • Understanding customer preferences
  • Identification of market trends

This helps companies improve product development and business strategies.

Key Insights and Findings

  • Top brands consistently maintain high product rankings.
  • Many cosmetic products support multiple skin types.
  • Price does not always determine product performance.
  • Customer preferences vary by skin suitability.
  • Data visualization helps understand trends easily.
  • Businesses can improve product strategy using insights.
  • Interactive dashboards improve decision making.

These insights help cosmetic companies enhance product quality, marketing strategies, and customer satisfaction.
